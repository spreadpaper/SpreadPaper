A: Sheet Modal (below sidebar)

Click a row → macOS sheet slides down over the sidebar area. Big range bar, editable name, large time display. Row stays highlighted while editing. Sheet has Done button to close.

B: Inline Expand (accordion)

Click a row → it expands in-place to show the range bar, name field, and remove link. Other rows collapse. No modal, no context switch. Everything stays in the sidebar. Compact when collapsed, roomy when expanded. Click another row or the ▲ to collapse.
