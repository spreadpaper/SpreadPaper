A: Inline Editable Time

Each row has the thumbnail + an inline time field (hour : minute AM/PM). Click any part to edit directly. No popover, no separate editing area. The time IS the row. Delete with ✕. Simple, direct, scannable.

B: 24-Hour Clock Ring

Visual clock showing the full day cycle. Colored segments show when each image is active. Drag dots around the ring to adjust times. Tap a segment to select that image. Center shows the selected time. Visually maps the concept of "wallpaper changes throughout the day."

C: Day Bar with Draggable Markers

Horizontal bar representing 24 hours with a day/night gradient. Draggable markers for each image. Thumbnails above the bar, selected image details below. Immediately communicates "this image shows at this time of day." Drag to reposition.
