A: Thumbnails on the Bar

Thumbnails sit directly above their position on the day bar, connected by a stem line. Drag the thumbnail to change its time. Selected image shows an editable "From" time below. The bar IS the timeline — thumbnails and time positions are unified in one visualization.

B: Segmented Day Bar

The bar itself is divided into colored segments representing each image's active period. Segment width = how long that image shows. Drag the dividers between segments to adjust times. Time labels inside each segment. Immediately shows proportional coverage — you can SEE that the night image covers 9 hours while the noon image covers 6.
